# Scriptures Audio Library

#### Original Audio

The foundation of our audio assets is a historic 1976 recording featuring Abraham Shmuelov's native Sephardic pronunciation reading directly from the Leningrad Codex. This authentic recording serves as the canonical source, preserved in its original state without alterations, capturing the precise Sephardic pronunciation tradition contemporary to its time.

#### Cloned Audio

This master recording was enhanced using AI technology to remove background noise, coughs, and minor errors, then cloned via ElevenLabs to produce a deeper, calmer, more professional voice while preserving the authentic Sephardic pronunciation. The cloned voice serves as the production standard across all media outputs.
