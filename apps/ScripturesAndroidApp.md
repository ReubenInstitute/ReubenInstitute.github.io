# Scriptures app for Android

The Scriptures app presents the full text of the Hebrew Bible (Tanakh) in an elegant, meticulously formatted Hebrew edition. The text is sourced directly from the *Leningrad Codex* and adheres to all of the Institute’s typographic standards—poetic layout, modern punctuation, and custom font features that emphasise *Sheva Na*, *Dagesh Hazzak*, and *Qamats Katan*.  

Audio is currently available for the complete Book of Psalms and for several Torah portions (Parashot), using the authentic 1976 Sephardic pronunciation recording of Abraham Shmuelov. The audio has been refined with AI technology to remove background noise while preserving the original vocal character.

## Core Experience

A clean, right‑to‑left Hebrew Bible browser with free access to the full text and an audio‑experience system that rewards regular use. The initial release covers the Hebrew Bible; future expansions will add the Mishnah, Talmuds, Zohar, and other core texts from the Institute’s Scriptures library.

## Key Features

1. ### Complete Hebrew Bible
   All 24 books (Torah, Nevi’im, Ketuvim) are present, with the text rendered in lyrical verses—not rectangular prose blocks—using phrase‑by‑phrase line breaks that mirror natural recitation pauses. Modern punctuation (including curly quotes, em dashes, and semicolons) is applied at positions derived from the original cantillation marks.

2. ### Audio for Psalms and Selected Parashot
   *Full Psalms* – Every chapter is available as a high‑quality audio track, playable on‑demand.  
   *Selected Parashot* – A growing collection of weekly Torah portions is included, with the same authentic pronunciation.  
   Both are sourced from the original Shmuelov recording, cleaned and enhanced without altering the Sephardic vocalisation.

3. ### Intuitive Navigation
   * Swipe left/right between chapters.  
   * Quick‑jump menu with Hebrew book names and chapter numbers.  
   * Touch‑friendly navigation buttons.

4. ### Hebrew‑Centric Design
   * Right‑to‑left layout.  
   * Authentic Torah‑script font.  
   * Gematria chapter and verse numbers.  
   * Sacred name handling (יהוה).

5. ### Tiered Audio Access
   * **Free Tier** – Full reading access. Unlock audio through meaningful engagement (daily reading, challenges, etc.).  
   * **Pro Tier** – Unlimited offline audio for all available content (Psalms and Parashot), background playback, and auto‑scroll.

## Planned Expansions
- Additional Parashot and, eventually, the complete Torah audio.  
- Remaining books of the Tanakh (audio for all of Nevi’im and Ketuvim).  
- Integration of the wider Scriptures library (Mishnah, Talmuds, Zohar) as both text and audio become available.  
- Daily/weekly reading schedules mirroring the traditional divisions.
