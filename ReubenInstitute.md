# Reuben Institute

[LOGO](img/logo.png)

_**"obsession for accuracy"**_

Reuben Institute is a scholarly organization and publishing studio dedicated to disseminating
and publishing foundational Hebrew and Aramaic texts. Motivated by the prevalence of inaccuracies
in existing published editions, the Institute utilises ancient, public‑domain source materials to
produce meticulously curated content across **print**, **digital**, **audio**, and **video** formats.
By combining rigorous academic standards with modern technology, the Institute ensures that
historical traditions remain accessible and accurate for contemporary audiences.
