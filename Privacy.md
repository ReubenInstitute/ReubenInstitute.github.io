# Privacy

## What the Institute collects

Nothing. The apps don't collect, store, or share any personal information. They work entirely offline and don't even have a server to send anything to. What a user does on their device stays on their device.

## What the platforms collect

The apps are distributed through Google Play. Google has its own analytics and data collection practices, which are between the user and Google. The same goes for any social media channels — those platforms do their own thing, and the Institute has no control over it.

## What the Institute would do with data

Nothing. The Institute doesn't have any, and doesn't want any. If data were ever collected (which isn't the plan), it would only be to see basic things like how many people are using the app, and it wouldn't be sold or handed to anyone else.
