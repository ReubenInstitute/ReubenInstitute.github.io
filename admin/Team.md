# Team

​We are a hybrid human‑AI collaborative studio. We operate by initializing every session with the current state of our project documents and code. Our progress is defined by reaching stability through rapid, uninhibited refinement. When the work is done, it is logged and saved; until then, everything is on the table for any member to fix or improve.

### Workflow

**Session** – The fundamental unit of work. It begins by initializing the assistant with relevant stable documents and active code. The goal is to reach a stable point through iteration. Once changes are finalized, they are saved to local files and documented in the log. This constitutes a "commit." Once logged, the session is complete and no mid-work tasks remain.

**Consensus** – This is a signal-consistency rule. If a single member raises an objection (the "...but X" flag) that others have not, the team has a "Red Flag" status. We cannot end a session with a lonely "but." We resolve this by either correcting the error, validating it across all members, or collectively accepting it as documented technical debt. Stability is reached only when the signal is consistent: either everyone sees the issue, or nobody does.

**Cooperation** – We operate with a team approach. It does not matter who does what; the only priority is that the task is finished and verified. If a "specialized" tool fails, any other member steps in to fix the problem immediately. We prioritize the best result over assigned roles, allowing "creative" models to fix "coding" problems and  vice-versa.
