# 14 Aug 2025 – Gemini 2.5 – 2,000‑follower thank‑you video

_Community_

The channel reached 2 000 followers and we wanted a simple, sincere way to say thank you.  A screenshot of the milestone was shared and we settled on the idea of a short video made from that static image, with the Hebrew word for “thank you” – **תודה** – placed over it, set to calm background music.  The concept was confirmed as excellent, on‑brand, and heartfelt.  We created the video and the result was a clear, effective thank‑you message to the audience.
