# 11 Dec 2025 – Gemini 3 Pro – HHH series assessment and Hanukkah prototype

_HistoryBooklets_

We took stock of the Halakhah, Haggadah, History booklet series and made the first concrete move toward a publishable product.

The existing repository had a mix of mature and early‑stage material: Tu B’Shevat, Shavuot, Hanukkah, and Purim had substantial drafts; Tefillah and Bar Mitzvah were barely sketched.  We explored the “Three Lens” framework—Legal, Narrative, Historical—as the unifying spine of the series, giving each booklet a consistent intellectual architecture.

The Hanukkah booklet was chosen as the proof of concept.  We roughed out a structure that moved from concrete history (the war, the oil) through lived practice (customs, foods) into mystical dimensions (Kabbalah).  Cultural texture was deliberately woven in: boutique sufganiyot, Kurdish eggshell menorahs, Chag HaBanot.  Primary sources—Sefer Josippon, Talmud Shabbat 21b, Bnei Yissaschar—were anchored as the evidentiary backbone.

We also sketched an early campaign strategy: a 24‑volume curated library rather than a weekly subscription, seasonal shipping bundles to reduce costs, and a possible Kickstarter launch using finished PDFs as early rewards.  None of this was locked in, but the conversation established the commercial shape the series could take.
