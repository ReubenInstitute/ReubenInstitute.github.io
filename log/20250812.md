# 12 Aug 2025 – Gemini 2.5 – Psalms project foundation and subscription model

_Business_

The session transformed the Psalms Project from a simple content series into a two‑tiered, monetisable service built around a global audience that had arrived unexpectedly.

### The foundation

The project rests on three core assets: a custom, poetic Hebrew text derived from the Leningrad Codex; an authentic, AI‑enhanced 1976 Sephardic audio recording; and a professional, automated system that produces daily vertical videos for TikTok, Instagram, and YouTube.

### The opportunity

Analytics revealed a spike from roughly 200 followers to 3 200, driven by two viral events.  The breakout brought in a massive international audience, particularly from Latin America, despite the videos containing only Hebrew text and audio.  Those new followers were actively asking for translations.

### New capabilities

To answer that demand, the Institute developed two new assets:
* A modern English translation of the Psalms, based on the JPS 1917 version.
* AI‑generated English audio for each verse.

### Two video formats

We designed two distinct formats to serve different audiences:
* **Gateway Video** – the public‑facing standard.  Hebrew text and Hebrew audio, with the English translation displayed as a courtesy subtitle.  It maintains the original aesthetic while making the content accessible to non‑Hebrew speakers.
* **Premium Video** – the flagship product.  Hebrew and English texts displayed simultaneously, with a unique audio track that plays the Hebrew audio followed by the English audio for each verse.  A complete, side‑by‑side experience.

### Subscription and release model

A transparent freemium strategy was adopted:
* **Free**: daily Gateway videos, plus a select few Premium videos released publicly as a preview.
* **Paid**: daily, immediate access to all Premium videos; the full library of all 150 Psalms; and all current and future enhancements (new visuals, new translations).  The subscription is a membership in a living project, not a one‑time purchase.

### Keyword strategy

To ensure each format reaches its intended audience:
* **Gateway videos** use primarily Hebrew keywords and hashtags, continuing to target the core Hebrew‑speaking audience.
* **Premium videos** use a strategic mix of Hebrew, English, and Spanish/Portuguese keywords (e.g. `#Salmos`, `#Biblia`) to actively attract the new international audience.

The plan turned the Psalms Project into a dynamic, two‑tiered service designed to serve and monetise both the original audience and the newly discovered global one.
