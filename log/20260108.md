# 8 Jan 2026 – Gemini 3 Flash – Asset Vault with UUID and DNA workflow

_Media_

The Reuben Institute transitioned from a creative concept into a structured production house with a “Time Machine” database and a “DNA‑based” workflow that tracks every visual asset without losing a frame or a job.

### The Genetic workflow

A strict three‑step lineage ensures every asset is traceable and reproducible:
1. **Square DNA** — a 1:1 image that locks in the composition, the “soul” of the prompt.
2. **SD Branching** — from the Square, two SD versions are created: Portrait (9:16) and Landscape (16:9), the foundational blueprints.
3. **Final Video** — the SD images are extended into video clips.

### Format and quality standards

Vertical (Portrait) is produced as SD video for fast, efficient mobile delivery.  Horizontal (Landscape) is produced as HD video — high‑fidelity “Master” versions for cinematic use.  Both formats are kept at identical duration (e.g. 12.9 seconds), creating format‑agnostic pairs that can be swapped seamlessly in editing.

### The Lean Asset Vault

A custom Markdown table acts as a searchable index for the local hard drive.  Bulky identifiers like Job ID or URL are stripped away.  Simple descriptors — square, portrait, landscape — serve as clickable links pointing directly to Midjourney job pages.  For final video files, the last 5–6 characters of the unique ID (e.g. `c5c66`) serve as the search key; Midjourney includes this fragment in the downloaded filename, so any clip on disk can be instantly matched to its source in the table.

### The Ghost Ship Rule

A directorial policy was solidified for AI hallucinations.  If the AI adds unwanted elements — the infamous ship’s rope in the ocean, for instance — the segment is discarded immediately, the lineage is rewound to the last clean Job ID, and the extension is re‑rolled.  This ensures the “Invisible POV” of the Institute remains consistent across all 150 Psalms.

### Document integration

The tables were integrated into the beginning of the `Psalms.md` master draft, creating a Production Bible where spiritual themes, chapter breakdowns, and technical job IDs coexist in a single, high‑performance document.  Row 1 — The Calm Ocean — is officially “Mastered,” providing a perfect template for the Fog, Rain, and Mountain segments to come.
