# 18 Dec 2025 – DeepSeek 3 – Video asset system review and music generation

_Media_

The asset management system was reviewed and extended, and a complete music generation pipeline was built from scratch.

### Video asset system

The structured markdown inventory for AI‑generated video backgrounds was examined and validated.  The system categorises clips by type (Lake, Ocean, Forest Path, etc.) with Gemini‑generated descriptions, tracks assignments to specific Psalms, and provides instant visibility into which assets are available and which are already in use.  The traceability — prompt → filename → description → usage — makes asset rotation efficient and prevents duplicates.

### Music generation pipeline

A prompt‑based system was established for generating ambient music tracks to score the Psalms.  The prompts combine keys and modes — mapped to Psalm types (lament, wisdom, praise) — with specific instruments: drone pads, low strings, harp, ney, and waterphone.  The constraints are strict: serious, meditative, reverent, with no rhythm and no melody.  Every decision is documented in `Suno.md`.

### File management automation

Suno’s export system appends `(1)`, `(2)` to duplicate filenames, which breaks batch processing.  A script was written to strip those markers, append the file’s creation timestamp (with milliseconds for uniqueness), and rename in place, producing clean filenames like `PromptName-YYYYMMDD-HHMMSS.MMM.mp3`.

### Track analysis and inventory

Gemini was used to analyse the generated tracks — unbiased descriptions of each track’s mood, texture, and emotional character — formatted for direct inclusion in a music inventory markdown file.  Durations were obtained via an `ffprobe` script.

### Regeneration strategy

Based on the analysis, clear priorities emerged.  The `Emin LowStrings Ney Harp` and `Phrygian Ney LowStrings` prompts produced the most successful and distinct tracks (cinematic/earthy and ancient/spiritual respectively).  The `Ambiguous DronePads` and `Bbmin DronePads Harp` prompts were flagged for regeneration to achieve greater emotional range and better fit their intended Psalm categories.

The session transformed music from a one‑off experiment into a systematic, documented production pipeline.
