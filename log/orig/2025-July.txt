#### Jul 2025 – DeepSeek 3 – Ancient history

December 2023: Our journey began with creating sequential, thematic "episodes" for each Parashah. These videos overlaid verse text on a looping, color-tinted background and were uploaded to TikTok more as a storage exercise. We produced over 15 such Parashot.
December 2024: We experimented with Luma AI, generating images for a full 10-episode series of the Parasha of Bereishit. The images were created directly from the Hebrew verse text, and this approach saw a modest increase in views, with one video reaching 6,000.
July 2025: After a period of no activity, we returned with a new focus on Psalms. Our first video in this new style, featuring the original voice and a long, side-scrolling background image made with Luma AI, gained significant traction and has now reached 38,000 views.
End of July 2025: We launched a regular series featuring an improved AI-cloned voice and a consistent zoom-in-through-fog background. This new strategy immediately led to a viral event, with our first video in this format reaching 76,000 views and a rapid increase in followers from Latin-speaking countries, particularly Brazil.
