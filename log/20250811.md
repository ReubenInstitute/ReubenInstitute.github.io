# 11 Aug 2025 – Gemini 2.5 – Psalms background concepts refined

_Media_

The session mapped the visual foundation of the Psalms project: a library of serene, looping background videos designed to be non‑distracting carriers of sacred text, generated through Midjourney for stills and Kling for animation.

We started with a foundation of six concepts, each with a detailed Midjourney image prompt and a Kling video prompt:
* **Clouds** – serene
* **Stormy Ocean** – dramatic
* **Desert Dunes** – serene
* **Fields of Grass** – idyllic
* **Floating Through Clouds** – ethereal
* **Golden Wheat Field** – peaceful and abundant

The heart of the session was refining a seventh concept to expand the emotional range.  The goal was a faster‑paced, energetic alternative to the slow serenity of the Golden Wheat Field.

### From “Wild” to “Vast Manicured Grass”

The initial idea of “Wild Overgrown Grass” evoked energy and freedom, but it leaned toward abandonment – an aesthetic that didn’t sit comfortably with the sacred, intentional nature of the Psalms.  The shift to **Vast Manicured Grass** changed the emotional register entirely: from wildness to joyful purpose, from chaos to freedom within order.

### Precision in language

We then sharpened every word of the prompts to guide the AI toward the exact visual we wanted:
* Removed the word “field” when describing the wind’s action, connecting the motion directly to the grass itself – “a soft breeze makes the grass sway.”
* Eliminated “blade” as too sharp and technical, replacing it with language that describes the collective, soft movement of the grass as a whole: “verdant surface,” “stalk,” “ripple.”

### Defined purpose

The new background was earmarked specifically for Psalms that are celebratory, triumphant, or express passionate yearning and confident movement.

The outcome was a nuanced seventh background concept – energetic, medium‑speed motion; orderly beauty and purposeful joy; and precisely crafted prompts that use intentional language to avoid unwanted connotations and guide the AI toward a majestic, controlled aesthetic.  The broader lesson was that a single word shift in a prompt could change the entire emotional atmosphere of a video.
