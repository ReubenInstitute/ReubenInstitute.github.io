# 9 Jan 2026 – DeepSeek 3 – Psalm 8 cinematic pipeline and TikTok duet

_Media, Social_

A complete, end‑to‑end test of the media production pipeline turned a single Markdown table row into a published TikTok video — and organic user‑generated content validated the format’s real‑world utility.

### From prompt to storyboard

What began as a “calm ocean” entry in an asset table expanded into a full cinematic storyboard for Psalm 8: seven distinct shots (POV and aerial) with durations assigned, two of them flagged for speed adjustments (0.7× and 0.8×) as the first application of the Stealth Reusability Matrix.

### Automated asset processing

A Python script (`rename.py`) was written to process the downloaded assets.  When direct HTTP downloads failed due to Stealth Mode permissions, we adapted by using Midjourney’s folder download feature.  The script renamed the files to `1.mp4` through `7.mp4` and applied precise speed adjustments with `ffmpeg-python` at a CRF of 18 for optimal quality preservation.

### Composition and publication

The processed clips were composited, the AI‑composed Suno music track was added, and the enhanced video was generated through the studio’s production tools.  The finished Psalm 8 was published to TikTok.

### Validation

Gemini analysed the final MP4 without any context and correctly identified its theological themes, cinematic visual language, and auditory atmosphere — an objective confirmation that the pipeline’s output communicates what it intends.  On TikTok, the video showed strong initial engagement and the characteristic “camel‑back” viewership pattern across global time zones.

### Organic user‑generated content

A user, David Ming, created a duet using our video as a background to practise and recite Psalm 117 in Hebrew.  This was unsolicited validation: our format works as a reusable template for active learning, embodying the universal call of the texts themselves.

The session proved that the full production pipeline — narrative planning, asset engineering, automated processing, final production, distribution, and impact validation — operates as a single, reliable system.
