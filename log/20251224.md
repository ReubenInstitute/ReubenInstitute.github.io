# 24 Dec 2025 – DeepSeek 3 – “Essa Einai” music video production

_Piyutim, Media_

We took a creative concept — a desert campfire gathering — and turned it into a finished, professional‑grade music video that independently validated our entire AI production workflow.

### Concept and storyboard

The visual narrative was built from symbolic shots, each carrying a clear emotional beat:
* **Establishing Shot**: a slow drone flight over a vast, empty desert.
* **The Herald**: a lone, hooded nay player on a cliff under the stars.
* **The Source**: an aerial shot of the solitary singer by a small fire, arms raised.
* **The Gathering**: a solemn crowd encircling a fire.
* **The Climax**: an epic aerial view of concentric circles — musicians, a full orchestra, and a dancing crowd.

### Technical strategy

With five GPU hours remaining, we planned for Standard Definition renders and strategic segment lengths (12–16 seconds each).  The breakthrough was a “scene evolution” technique: generate one strong base image or video, then tweak the descriptive prompt — removing the orchestra, for example — to create consistent narrative stages.  This optimised both time and visual cohesion.

### Production

The plan was executed directly: concise, objective scene descriptions were written, fed to Midjourney to create base images, and those images were extended into video clips multiple times to reach the desired duration for each scene.

### Validation

Gemini analysed the final video without any context.  It correctly identified the core narrative journey — from solitude to community — and understood the key symbols (herald, fire, circles).  More importantly, it framed the AI‑generated aesthetic not as a limitation but as the feature that created a “timeless” and “epically spiritual” mood.

The project proved that a creative concept can move from idea to finished, emotionally resonant music video using a scalable AI workflow — and that the result can stand on its own, communicating profound symbolism without explanation.
