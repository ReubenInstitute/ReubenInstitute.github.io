
# 28 Dec 2025 – DeepSeek 3 – Cross‑platform strategy and Psalm 90 model cycle

_Social, Media_

We consolidated the Institute’s multi‑platform presence into a single, repeatable daily pipeline and used Psalm 90 as the proof that it works.

### Data and tracking cleanup

The chaotic tracking system was overhauled.  TikTok columns (C, E, O) were merged into one for clarity.  Version codes were standardised — `001C`, `001E`, `001H`, `001O` — and applied consistently across every Psalm.  YouTube and Instagram publishing status was updated for Psalms 1–17 (horizontal, clean), old‑series uploads (3, 10, 12, 15, 20, 30), and new‑series entries (35, 36, 41, 51).  The Psalms table was split by Book (1–5) for easier navigation, and the same structured tracking was applied to Piyutim.

### Cross‑platform rules

Clear, automated rules were defined for each platform: TikTok and Instagram get all versions (C + E daily), YouTube only gets videos under 4:00 (longer ones are substituted), Spotify receives enhanced audio with ID3 tags, and WhatsApp gets enhanced version notifications.  The nightly workflow is “finish and forget” — make C and E in the evening, schedule AM/PM posts everywhere, upload audio to Spotify, share to WhatsApp, update the tables, done.  No backlog, no overthinking, no platform gaps.

### Psalm 90 as the model cycle

Psalm 90 was chosen for its natural fit (2:05 duration, autumn mood, duduk accompaniment).  Both versions were created: `090C` (clean + fog) scheduled for 7:30 AM, `090E` (enhanced + autumn tunnel + duduk) scheduled for 7:30 PM.  Publishing followed the new rules across all platforms.  Gemini analysis confirmed thematic, visual, and musical cohesion — the system produced a coherent, professional product without manual intervention.

### Technical improvements

The refactored `PsalmCover` class, with its cleaner typography and shadow effects, was applied to all recent and relevant posts.  Landscape videos are officially on pause; vertical and shorts are the priority.

### What this means moving forward

The Institute now has a single, tight, repeatable process.  Clear tracking across five‑plus platforms.  Automated decision‑making — if a video exceeds four minutes, it skips YouTube.  Thematic asset pairing is systematic.  One Psalm per day, fully delivered, every day.
