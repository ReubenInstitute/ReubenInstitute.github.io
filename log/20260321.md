### The IVR (VoiceLine) ecosystem

What had been a vague description of a phone‑based study platform was transformed into a detailed operational guide:

* **The Weekly Quiz** — defined the "10+2" question structure, including "star" difficulty levels, weighted scoring, and a hard reset at 0:00 Sunday (Gregorian midnight).  The quiz logic was separated from the manual workflows of winner recognition and sponsorship management.
* **The "Handshake" security** — a low‑tech, foolproof verification system where winners must present their physical phone to a sponsor for a live SMS check to claim prizes.  No logins, no passwords, no digital attack surface.  Pure physical‑presence verification.
* **Operational items** — automated quiz logic and manual sponsorship administration were cleanly separated, so each can evolve independently.
