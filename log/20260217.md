# 17 Feb 2026 – Gemini 3 Pro – Psalm‑Align Editor foundation and BPM calibration

_Studio_

We started building a dedicated Psalm‑Align Editor to handle word‑level synchronisation between audio and text.  The foundation was a new `/align` route and a standalone `psalm-align.html` page.  Temporal resolution was fixed at 12 px/s — a deliberate choice that gave enough visual granularity for manual alignment without making the waveform unworkably wide.

The dashboard was built around four mathematical values: Beat duration, Bar duration, Gross Beat count, and Gross Bar count.  These gave us a numeric frame for calibrating playback speed against the source recording.  A multi‑subdivision BPM tapper widget was integrated so we could tap along with the music and derive a reliable tempo.  The full loop — ffprobe duration extraction, backend‑to‑frontend passing, and waveform display — was validated end‑to‑end so the alignment data would survive a page reload.
