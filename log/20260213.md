# 13 Feb 2026 – DeepSeek 3 – “Imageless” videos, evening/morning rhythm, remaining Tikun Haklali

_Media, PsalmSeries_

We rebranded the simple fog‑plus‑voice videos as “imageless” — a term that made their aesthetic intention explicit.  The publishing rhythm was formalised into a morning and evening pair, with the imageless version going out early and the enhanced version following later.  Psalm 66 served as the validation case, pulling 3 900 views and 52 shares with the new schedule, strong enough to lock the pattern in.

We also audited the Tikun Haklali set and identified four Psalms still missing from the production queue: 42, 105, 137, and 150.  For YouTube, we sketched a horizontal imageless strategy that would let those longer videos live on a platform better suited to extended watch times without requiring enhanced backgrounds.
