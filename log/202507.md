# Jul 2025 – DeepSeek 3 – Ancient history

_Documentation_

The Institute’s content journey began long before the viral breakout, with two early experiments that shaped the direction.

**December 2023** – We produced over 15 Parashot as sequential, thematic “episodes.” Each video overlaid verse text on a looping, colour‑tinted background and was uploaded to TikTok, primarily as a storage exercise rather than a serious audience play.

**December 2024** – We experimented with Luma AI, generating images directly from the Hebrew verse text for a full 10‑episode series of the Parasha of Bereishit. This approach saw a modest increase in views, with one video reaching 6 000.

**July 2025** – After a period of inactivity, we returned with a new focus on Psalms. The first video in the new style used the original voice and a long, side‑scrolling background image made with Luma AI. It gained significant traction, eventually reaching 38 000 views.

**End of July 2025** – We launched a regular series featuring an improved AI‑cloned voice and a consistent zoom‑through‑fog background. The first video in this format hit 76 000 views immediately, triggering a viral event and a rapid influx of followers from Latin‑speaking countries, particularly Brazil. This was the turning point that led to all the strategy and architecture work that followed.
