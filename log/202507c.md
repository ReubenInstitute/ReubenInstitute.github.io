# Jul 2025 – Gemini 2.5 – Two‑tiered audio strategy

_Media_

We established a two‑tiered audio production strategy that balances absolute quality with scalable output.

For **main videos** – the gold standard – the voice is cloned from a clean, high‑quality human recording, giving the most impressive and realistic result available.

For **additional informative videos**, the source is an AI‑generated voice.  Before cloning, this voice is processed through subtle effects (reverb, flanger, or delay) and then compressed.  The treatment masks the digital artifacts and inconsistencies inherent in AI‑generated audio, producing a plausible, realistic input for the cloning step.

The dual approach keeps pristine audio for core content while efficiently generating high‑quality supplementary material.
