# 26 Dec 2025 – DeepSeek 3 – Media strategy and production system

_Social, Media_

The Reuben Institute’s entire media operation was documented, systematised, and turned into a sustainable daily pipeline — the transition from experimental content creation to running a real production studio.

### TikTok inventory

A complete inventory of Psalms on TikTok was compiled: 34 Clean (fog + voice), 9 Enhanced (AI background + music), 15 Old, and 2 Multilingual.  Each version was assigned a code — C, E, O, M — and tracked in a master `Videos.md` dashboard.  The table makes gaps instantly visible and ensures nothing falls through the cracks.

### Sustainable workflow

The daily production cycle was refined to roughly 1.5 hours, producing both Clean and Enhanced versions of a single Psalm.  The key decision was random Psalm selection over sequential — it keeps the audience curious and prevents the feed from feeling predictable.  Posting follows a dual daily schedule: Clean at 7 AM, Enhanced at 7 PM, across all platforms.

The “finish and forget” philosophy is the backbone of the system: complete production and scheduling on the same day, zero backlog.  Every step, from audio marking to cross‑platform scheduling, is documented in a 10‑step workflow.

### Multi‑platform strategy

TikTok leads.  Instagram and YouTube receive the same content, mirrored, plus platform‑specific “catch‑up” posts of legacy material to fill their libraries.  The growth trajectory is clear: 5.6 K followers, 43 K weekly views (+362%), with a milestone target of 100 K views over 28 days to unlock TikTok subscriptions.
