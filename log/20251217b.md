# 17 Dec 2025 – DeepSeek 3 – Background video asset library creation

_Media_

A systematic, searchable inventory of AI‑generated background videos was created, turning a chaotic collection of clips into a professional asset management system.

### The workflow

The pipeline is repeatable and documented: generate videos via Midjourney with standardised prompts, process them through `filter.py` to enforce the washed‑out, low‑contrast aesthetic, use Gemini Vision to generate detailed descriptions, assign videos to Psalm numbers where they’re used, and document everything in structured markdown.

The standardised prompt template is `[Scene description], photorealistic, cinematic, atmospheric, serene, shallow depth of field, muted palette`.  The aesthetic parameters are deliberate: muted palette for the 70s‑photo look, shallow depth of field to help text readability, cinematic and atmospheric for consistent mood, and serene to match the spiritual tone.

### The inventory

Ten background videos were catalogued across five prompt categories: Lake (3 videos, one assigned to Psalm 41), Ocean (3 videos, one assigned to Psalm 70), Waterfall (1 video, assigned to Psalm 47), Forest Path (2 videos, unassigned), and Meadow (1 video, unassigned).

The document structure is clean: prompt definitions with standardised parameters, then each video tagged with its Psalm assignment, prompt category, filename, and a Gemini‑generated description.  The system provides complete traceability from prompt to filename to description to usage, consistency control through the shared filter pipeline, searchability by Psalm or prompt category, and scalability for new prompts and videos.

### Design synergy

The system creates a self‑reinforcing visual language: backgrounds are washed‑out and low‑contrast, text is yellow with a radial black shadow, and the result is text that pops without fighting the background while maintaining brand consistency across every video.

The asset library is production‑ready for daily TikTok content creation with efficient rotation and quality consistency.
