### Weekly Quiz (plan)

The Weekly Quiz is a study tool based on the current Parashah. It consists of 12 multiple-choice questions: 10 core questions and 2 optional "star" difficulty levels. The scoring is weighted to favor the higher-complexity questions. To prevent pattern memorization, the questions and answers are randomized for every session. Participation is tied to the user's Caller ID, allowing for the modification of answers throughout the week until the deadline, Saturday evening at 23:59:59 (Gregorian midnight).

#### Winner Recognition (plan)
This is the process of managing and announcing the weekly winners. It involves notifying the winner via SMS, capturing their preferred public details (Name, Age, and Location), and publishing that recognition in the following week’s quiz introduction.

#### Sponsorship & Rewards (plan)
This is the management of physical prizes donated by local entities (e.g., bookstores or Judaica shops). It includes integrating "Sponsored by" mentions into the quiz audio and managing the live verification process, where the sponsor confirms the winner’s identity by sending a live SMS to the physical phone used to participate in the quiz.
