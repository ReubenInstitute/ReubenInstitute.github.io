# Parashah Series

The **Parashah Series** follows the weekly *Torah* portion in its traditional annual cycle. It offers shorter daily sequential episodes, and the complete portion at the end of the week.

All episodes are produced under the Institute’s unified media framework:
- **Audio** is derived from historic recordings of Abraham Shmuelov, processed with AI voice cloning (ElevenLabs “Dark Knox 2”) to achieve a clean, studio-quality narration while preserving the authentic Eastern/Sephardic pronunciation.
- **Video** features synchronised text captions highlighting the verse or paragraph, with custom background imagery (raw versions use a neutral fog background).

**Distribution:** YouTube, YouTube Shorts, Instagram, TikTok, Spotify (audio-only), WhatsApp Channel, and the IVR phone line.
